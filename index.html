What we do

Alabama's Seventeenth Judicial Circuit Community Corrections Program is designed with the public's safety in mind. It offers the criminal justice system an opportunity to increase accountability, provide restitution to the victim, tailor a sentence to an offender's crime and rehabilitative needs, reduce the cost of punishment, and ensure that costly prison cells are reserved for those for whom the public needs protection from.

Adult Drug Court

Adult Drug Court is a twelve month, comprehensive substance abuse and court monitored program. Admission criteria: An individual must have a drug or drug related charge. Also, an individual must be at least 18 years old.

Alternative Sentencing

The Alternative Sentencing Program identifies felony offenders who can be punished safely within the community by utilizing sentencing options that range from probation to incarceration.

Pre-Trial Services

Pre-Trial Services identifies and assists defendants that are in jail and unable to post bond. The program primarily deals with nonviolent offenders whose cases are pending before the District Court or Grand Jury.

Court Referral

The Court Referral Program is a key component of Community Corrections. The program provides a comprehensive approach to the management of cases involving substance or related issues.

Mental Health Court

Mental Health Court is a voluntary comprehensive mental health, substance program. Criteria for admission includes: An individual must have a non-violent charge. An individual must also have a diagnosis of a serious mental illness as defined in the DSM-V.

Veterans Treatment Court

Veterans Treatment Court is a voluntary comprehensive mental health, substance abuse, and court-monitored program. Criteria for admission includes: An individual must have served or is currently serving in the Armed Forces of the United States. An individual must aso have a nonviolent charge.

Drug Testing

Drug Testing is a condition of each program. We provide standard urine testing panels from 5-10 drugs including alcohol. We also offer drug confirmations as requested. Drug Testing is administered by certified personnel.

Community Service

Community Service is a condition of each program. All defendants are court ordered to perform community service at an approved placement.
